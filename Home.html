1. Café Latte: A classic coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk, typically topped with a small amount of foam. At Starbucks, it's simply referred to as "Latte."
2. Caffè Americano: A beverage made by diluting espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to drip coffee but with a different flavor profile. At Starbucks, it's called "Americano."
3. Caffè Macchiato: Traditionally, a macchiato is an espresso "stained" with a small amount of milk or foam. At Starbucks, you'll find variations like the "Caramel Macchiato," which includes vanilla syrup, steamed milk, espresso, and caramel drizzle.
